About Me

I am a Computer Engineering student with a strong interest in technology development, ranging from programming and system design to the Internet of Things (IoT). Throughout my studies, I have learned to combine logic, problem-solving skills, and creativity to deliver efficient solutions. I believe that technology is not only about innovation but also about making a positive impact in everyday life.

Beyond the tech world, I am also a traditional artist who enjoys creating character illustrations and visual art. Using pencils, I bring ideas and emotions to life through detailed and story-driven artworks. For me, art is a way to express what words often cannot, while also serving as a source of inspiration for every creative project I work on.
